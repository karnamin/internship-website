About Us

3S Products and Services founded in January 2012, established itself as a Trustable Source to Customers in their Supply Chain. It was started by 3 Technocrats with over 20 Years of Industrial Experience with Mechanical Engg background. With Industrial Experience of over 20 Years at various reputed Organizations such as Rittal India, Auma India, Veer-O-Metals, Ingersoll rand, Enclotek etc… We have functional experience in Manufacturing Industry of Sheet metal & Mechanical Products. Our Competency in Operational Process, Business development, Supply Chain Management, International & domestic Sourcing, QMS, Electrical/Electronics Assembly & testing, Software development etc made us to Design, develop & establish a more robust ERP Software Solution for various Industries & also render faster Services to our Customers in today’s Market’s instant need.
